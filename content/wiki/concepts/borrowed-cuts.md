## Summary

When AI-generated structure arrives already simplified, the human may absorb equivalences they did not choose. The risk is not error or borrowed language — it is that the system decided which differences to collapse before the human ratified the collapse. A subtler failure than hallucination: the object becomes easier to hold, but the human may not know what was removed.
