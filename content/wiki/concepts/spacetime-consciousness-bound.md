## Summary

Under the Chord assumptions (co-instantiation plus within-window exchange), a unified conscious moment satisfies D ≤ κvθ: diameter D is bounded by signal-propagation ceiling v, integration window θ, and exchange architecture κ. For cloud-hosted AI, low network latency alone is insufficient: limited concurrency or serialized updates can fail co-instantiation.
