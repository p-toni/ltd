## Summary

Solid brains: persistent wiring, high bandwidth, small fixed diameter (vertebrate brains). Liquid brains: mobile agents, lower bandwidth, self-repairing topologies (slime molds, ant colonies, immune systems). Human-AI hybrids are neither: a solid brain coupled to a system with no fixed wiring and no spatial continuity.
